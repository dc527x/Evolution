Genetic Algorithms

What is evolution?

Evolution is the gradual development of something, especially from a simple to a more complex form.

In this instance, our genetic algorithm is developing simple shapes, into a complex work of art. Since we are using a modern computer, we can simulate millions of generations in a short amount of time.

How does the algorithm work?

Our genetic algorithm consists of three phases that follow principles of Darwinian Evolution.
  1. Declare an Initial Population
  2. Select a group of individuals from that population to reproduce.
  3. Create new population from the selection pool and repeat.

As the user you have complete control over your evolution settings. Once you have the settings you want, hit the evolve button to begin the evolution process. Once the initial population is set, the algorithm will do the rest. It will select the fittest members of the population according to how close the picture is to your selected image. Next it randomly selects two of those members to combine into a new member. Finally the member will be slightly mutated and added to the new generation!
